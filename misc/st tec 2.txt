How it flows in practice:

Customer visits your site (GitHub Pages).

Frontend calls Cloudflare Worker for checkout or order info.

Worker:

Creates a Stripe session (Payment Element or QR code).

If physical product → creates Printful order.

Stores order info in KV.

Stripe handles the payment.

Printful fulfills physical product.

KV stores all order and session info.

Emails sent using Titan (optional confirmations, notifications).
